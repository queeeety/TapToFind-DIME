About Us

Our Mission
Our aim is to revolutionise the way we interact with music, from finding existing songs, to creating entirely new music, all while working to reduce plagiarism in the music industry

Our Story
The inspiration for our app struck when we noticed the detrimental effect that music generated by Artificial Intelligence is having on musicians. We wanted to make an app that would provide artists with song-writing tools, making music more accessible for all, and allowing artists to receive credit for their work

Our app will provide you with a rich variety of music tools,
changing the way you interact with and make music.

With our product you can..
•Tap to find the name and lyrics of your forgotten favourite songs
•Save beats on the go, wherever inspiration strikes
•Get intelligent AI recommendations for instruments, genre, rhythm and more, streamlining the music creation process
•And so much more..

Discover a completely new way of interacting with and creating music.

So what are you waiting for? Take the leap into a new era of music and discover all that our product has to offer!
