Look why we are unique

That’s Shazam. You all used it to find the name of the song that was playing near you.
It does require the song playing clearly to be able to identify it.
MusicTap doesn’t.

You don’t need to record a song. Just hum it to yourself and tap the rhythm – it’s as easy as sounds.

You can also hum it to Google,
but that require you to have Google Assistant,
it doesn’t always work good,
and we can imagine you in the crowded place trying to hum something to your phone.

Just tap it. We’ll take care of the rest.

If you are professional musician, there are some apps for you to use, also including AI, but they won’t help you with:

– Plagiarism analysis
– AI assistance in finishing a song saved in a project
– Built-in support for authors - not only tools, but also advice
– API model for developers

We will.

We are combining search + creativity + protection of rights in a single platform.
